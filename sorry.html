Shayari for MERI JAAAAAN❤️

Tere pyar ka ehsaas meri zindagi ka noor hai, 🌟✨
Tere efforts ke bina, sab kuch adhura aur door hai. 💔🌌
Teri muskaan se sajti hai meri har subah ki baatein, 😊🌞
Tere khayalon mein hi beeti hain meri raatin. 🌙💭



Tune mere liye apne sapne bhi thode diye, 🌈💖
Mere din ke liye apne khud ke din chhode diye. 💕⏳
Teri har baat mein chhupa hai ek gehra pyaar, 💌💘
Tere care se hi banti hai meri duniya har baar. 🌍💑



Main shayad turant nahi samajh paaya tera woh pyar, 🤔💞
Par tu hai meri zindagi ka sabse anmol sansar. 🏞️👩‍❤️‍👨
Tujhe dekh kar hi hota hai dil ka sukoon, 💓🥰
Tere bina toh har khushi hai ek udasi ka junoon. 💔🌧️



Tujhse wada hai, teri care ki kadar har pal karunga, 🤝❤️
Tere pyar ke safar mein kabhi peeche nahi hatunga. 🚶‍♂️➡️💑
Meri jaan, tu hai meri duniya ka sabse khubsurat hissa, 👸🌺
Tujhse door hona meri rooh ka sabse bada kissa. 😢🖤



Tere saath har lamha suhana hai, 🕰️✨
Meri har dua mein bas tera afsana hai. 🙏🌹
Teri efforts, tera pyar, aur tera care ka jahan, 🌌💕🌟
Tu hai meri zindagi ka asli asman! ❤️💫

*Inspired by some poets but written with my whole heart ❤️*

With all my love, Ayeshu bccha ❤️
